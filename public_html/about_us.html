Overview

King IT Limited is a renowned organization in Bangladesh. The activities of the company are Outsourcing, ICT Training, Skill Development, E-Comers Service and Software Solution House in Dhaka, Bangladesh, which has more than 3 years of experience in IT field. A highly skilled management team and well-qualified Board of Directors manage the affairs of the company. All projects in turnkey basis are managed and controlled by a strong project management team comprising of a Project Director, Technical Director, Project Manager and Co-ordinator. In addition to the members of the Board, the management is being supported by a combination of well-groomed and skilled IT, administration, finance, research and marketing staff members.

Our goal is to develop successful, long-term customers where our services integrate with specific technical needs. To achieve our goal, the company will maintain a marketing assurance of growing together with our clients.

  • To be the number one IT Company in Bangladesh within five years.
